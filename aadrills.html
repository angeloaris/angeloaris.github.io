Block Reversal:

Slot 1 - Forward Jump MK

4 - Whiff AA

Slot 1 - Forward Jump HK

Slot 2 - Crouching Heavy Kick

Drill: Hadoken distance and do either AA or 2MK to punish whiff 5 times on both sides.
